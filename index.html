Our History

Our organization was founded in 1887, not long after the first motor vehicle with an internal combustion engine was commercially produced. Our founder, Sir Quincy Longfellow, was a psychic that could immediatly predict the impact on the evironment that the motor vehicle industry would have. He started up Trees R Us and began protesting. Nobody understood what he was talking about at the time, but now, over a century later, it is very clear that he was right all along.

Our Goal

It is our goal to carry on Sir Longfellow's legacy and save the Earth.
